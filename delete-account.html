Delete your Defensive Pedal account

Effective date: 23 April 2026 · Last updated: 23 April 2026

This page explains how to permanently delete your Defensive Pedal account and the personal data associated with it. Defensive Pedal is operated by Antifragil SRL ("Antifragil", "we", "us"). Deleting your account is free, requires no justification, and can be requested at any time.

Summary. Send an email from the address registered to your Defensive Pedal account to victor@defensivepedal.com with the subject "Delete my Defensive Pedal account". We will confirm within 5 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days.

How to request account deletion — step by step

  1. Open your email application and start a new message from the email address associated with your Defensive Pedal account (the same address you use to sign in, either directly or via Google).
  2. Address the email to victor@defensivepedal.com.
  3. Use the subject line: Delete my Defensive Pedal account.
  4. In the body of the email, include the sentence: "I request the permanent deletion of my Defensive Pedal account and all associated personal data."
  5. (Optional) If you also want your publicly visible community content — shared trips, hazard reports, and comments — removed immediately, mention that in the same email. Otherwise it will be deleted together with your account.
  6. Send the email.

We will reply within 5 business days to confirm that we have received your request and to verify that the email was sent from the account holder. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of that confirmation.

What data is deleted

When we delete your account, the following categories of personal data are permanently erased from our production systems within 30 days:

Data category Examples
Account credentials Email address, bcrypt password hash (for email/password accounts), Google account identifier (for OAuth accounts), authentication tokens, verification records.
Profile information Display name, profile picture, preferences (theme, units, default bike type, map POI visibility, notification preferences, quiet hours).
Trip history Route records, breadcrumb GPS tracks, derived metrics (distance, duration, safety score, elevation profile), XP history, streaks, badge unlocks, tier, leaderboard entries.
Community content Hazard reports you submitted, hazard votes and confirmations, shared trips, likes and reactions, comments, post-ride feedback and ratings.
Device and technical data linked to your account Push notification token, device model and OS version associated with your account, app version, preferred language, crash logs linked to your account identifier.

What data is kept and for how long

A small amount of data is retained beyond account deletion, either because we are legally required to keep it or because it has already been irreversibly de-identified so it no longer relates to you. This data cannot be used to re-identify you.

Data category Retention period Reason
Aggregated and anonymized statistics Indefinite Used for safety research and service improvement. Personal identifiers are removed; this data cannot be linked back to you.
Anonymized crash logs and diagnostic data Up to 90 days After this period, crash data is either aggregated or deleted. Crash data linked to your account is removed together with the account.
Legal, financial, and tax records (if any) Up to 5 years Retained only where required by Romanian law, for example, proof of paid transactions or compliance with accounting obligations. Not applicable if you never made a payment.
Backup snapshots Up to 30 days after account deletion Encrypted database backups may still contain your data until the backup rotation window closes. Backups are not used for any operational purpose and are overwritten on schedule.

Deleting individual trips without deleting your account

If you want to remove a specific trip but keep using Defensive Pedal, you can do it from inside the app without contacting us:

  1. Open the Defensive Pedal app.
  2. Go to the History section from the bottom navigation bar, then tap Trips.
  3. Open the trip you want to delete.
  4. Tap the delete option on the trip detail screen and confirm.

The trip and its breadcrumb track will be removed from your history and from the community feed (if it was shared) within 30 days.

What happens after deletion

  • You will no longer be able to sign in with the deleted account.
  • If you want to use Defensive Pedal again, you will need to create a new account. Your previous trips, progression, badges, and leaderboard position cannot be restored.
  • Any public community content (shared trips, hazard reports, comments) attributed to your account is removed from the community feed.

Your rights under the GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, account deletion is one way to exercise your right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") under Article 17 of the GDPR. You also have the right to access your data, to correct it, to restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. For details on all your rights and how to exercise them, please see our Privacy Policy.

If you believe your data has not been handled in accordance with the GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Romanian supervisory authority, the National Supervisory Authority for Personal Data Processing (ANSPDCP) — www.dataprotection.ro.
